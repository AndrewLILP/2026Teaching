What is Sunday Drive?

Slow down. Look around.

Sunday Drive is a relaxing exploration game set in the golden landscapes of rural Australia. Drive through sun-drenched wine regions and eucalyptus scrubland, watch the world quietly come to life around you, and discover the extraordinary wildlife that calls this land home.

There's no score. No timer. No way to lose. Just the road ahead, the afternoon light, and whatever you find along the way.

Why Play

Built around four simple ideas

🛣️

Relaxation

No stress, no failure states, no time pressure. Sunday Drive is a space to breathe — made for old souls who want to unwind after the work week.

🔭

Discovery

The more you explore, the more the world reveals itself. Buildings emerge, new roads open up, and the landscape quietly fills with life.

🦘

Education

Encounter real Australian wildlife — kangaroos, emus, echidnas, platypus, and more — and learn fascinating facts about each species.

🌿

Growth

Watch the world grow with you. Like a city builder in slow motion, each visit makes the environment richer, warmer, and more alive.

The Dev Story

Made by one person, for a feeling.

Sunday Drive started with a simple question: what if a video game felt like that perfect Sunday afternoon drive — no agenda, no destination, just the road and the light?

Built solo in Unity 6, with deep personal knowledge of the Hunter Valley and Riverina regions, the game is crafted to capture something genuinely Australian — not a postcard version of it, but the real thing. The golden hour on the highway. The big sky. The moments when a kangaroo just hops into frame.

🎮

Phase 1 is live — a free WebGL demo is playable right now on itch.io. Phase 2 brings the Steam release with a full city scene, animated wildlife AI, and the complete outback world.

🇦🇺

Made in Australia — every building, landscape and animal behaviour is researched and built with authentic knowledge of the places it depicts.

🌐

Two versions — a browser build for instant play (itch.io) and a Windows PC release coming to Steam.
