NESA Syllabus Aligned

All content follows the Mathematics K–10 (2022), Technology 7–8 (2023), and Computing Technology 7–10 (2022) syllabuses. Outcome codes are shown throughout.

IB-Style Inquiry

Each chapter opens with a Statement of Inquiry, key and related concepts, and ATL skills — framing mathematics as a way of thinking, not just calculating.

Stage-Based, Not Year-Based

Content is organised by stage as NESA intends, allowing flexible delivery across Years 7–8 (Stage 4) without prescribing a fixed Year 7 or Year 8 split.
