Game Designer's Lens

🎮 Why Game Designers Need This Maths

Every game you've played was built on mathematical relationships. The distribution of items in Mario Kart is a ratio problem — designers carefully balance the odds so the race stays fair but exciting. The speed of a car in Forza is a rate, converted from real-world data. The mini-map path a character walks in Zelda can be modelled as a distance–time graph. This unit isn't just about maths — it's about the invisible mathematics that makes games feel right.

⚖️ Balance 📈 Progression curves 🎲 Probability & fairness 🔢 Stat scaling 🏎️ Physics simulation
