Stage 4 Maths  /  Measurement and Space

Measurement and Space

Measure and calculate lengths, areas and volumes; explore angle relationships, properties of shapes and apply Pythagoras' theorem in context.

6 outcomes
MA4-LEN-C-01
Length
Applies knowledge of the perimeter of plane shapes and the circumference of circles to solve problems.
Perimeter of polygons Circumference of circles Arc length Units and conversion Composite shapes
MA4-ARE-C-01
Area
Applies knowledge of area and composite area involving triangles, quadrilaterals and circles to solve problems.
Triangles Rectangles & parallelograms Trapeziums & rhombuses Circles and sectors Composite shapes
MA4-VOL-C-01
Volume and capacity
Applies knowledge of volume and capacity to solve problems involving right prisms and cylinders.
Volume of prisms Volume of cylinders Cross-sectional area Capacity conversions Composite solids
MA4-ANG-C-01
Angle relationships
Identifies and applies angle relationships related to intersecting lines, parallel lines and transversals to solve problems.
Angles at a point Vertically opposite Parallel lines & transversals Corresponding angles Alternate angles Co-interior angles
MA4-GEO-C-01
Properties of geometrical figures
Classifies and uses the properties of triangles and quadrilaterals to solve problems, developing geometric reasoning.
Triangle properties Quadrilateral properties Angle sum theorems Congruence Similarity Geometric proofs
MA4-PYT-C-01
Pythagoras' theorem
Applies Pythagoras' theorem to find unknown sides in right-angled triangles and solve problems in various contexts.
Hypotenuse Finding unknown sides Pythagorean triads Testing for right angles Real-world applications
