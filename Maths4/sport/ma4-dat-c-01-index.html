Data Classification & Visualisation

Stage 4 Mathematics · Years 7–8 · NSW Syllabus · Three interactive chapters covering classification, display and interpretation of data — all through Australian sport.

NSW Syllabus Outcome
MA4-DAT-C-01
Classifies and displays data using a variety of graphical representations.
Also addresses: MAO-WM-01 — Working mathematically develops understanding and fluency through exploring and connecting mathematical concepts, choosing and applying techniques to solve problems, and communicating thinking and reasoning coherently.

Working Mathematically — MAO-WM-01

Each chapter develops these skills through the arc activities, practise questions and journal prompts.

🔗 Connecting

Linking variable type to graph choice — and graph choice to conclusions that are valid.

🛠 Applying

Classifying real sports variables, constructing labelled graphs, selecting the right display for a purpose.

💬 Communicating

Justifying graph choices, interpreting patterns in data, explaining why a graph misleads.

For teachers: Each chapter follows the Hook → Explore → Learn → Practise → Reflect arc format. MCQ questions use tiered difficulty (Foundation / Core / Extension). Journal responses are saved to localStorage using the key prefix dat-ch1-, dat-ch2-, dat-ch3-. All files are self-contained and work offline.
