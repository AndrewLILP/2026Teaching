Course outcomes

CT5-OPL-01

Designs, produces and evaluates algorithms and implements them in a general-purpose programming language

CT5-DES-01

Designs and creates user interfaces and the user experience

CT5-DAT-01

Explains how data is stored, transmitted and secured in digital systems

CT5-DPM-01

Applies iterative processes to define problems and plan, design, develop and evaluate computing solutions

CT5-SAF-01

Selects and applies safe, secure and responsible practices in the ethical use of data and computing technology

CT5-EVL-01

Understands how innovation, enterprise and automation have inspired the evolution of computing technology

CT5-COL-01

Manages, documents and explains individual and collaborative work practices

CT5-COM-01

Communicates ideas, processes and solutions using appropriate media

Stage 5 · Year 10

Course Performance Descriptors

A

Extensive understanding of enterprise, innovation & automation. Skilfully applies iterative processes producing highly effective solutions. Technically concise algorithms, selects safe/secure/ethical data practices. Creativity and innovation in UI/UX; relevant data, media and processes across a range of contexts.

B

Thorough understanding of enterprise, innovation & automation. Applies iterative processes producing effective solutions using all three thinking types. Develops, tests and implements functional algorithms; selects & applies safe practices. Demonstrates creativity in UI/UX design.

C

Sound understanding of enterprise, innovation & automation. Applies iterative processes; develops sound solutions using computational or design or systems thinking. Implements common algorithms; applies safe, secure and ethical data practices. Designs and implements user interfaces.

D

Basic understanding of enterprise and innovation. Uses processes to produce solutions; develops basic solutions using one thinking type. Basic algorithms implemented in a general-purpose language; uses data safely and responsibly. Implements basic UI elements.

E

Identifies evolution of and/or innovations in computing technology. Identifies processes that may produce a computing solution. Follows algorithms and/or partially implements them. Works safely with data; identifies UI elements that contribute to user experience.
