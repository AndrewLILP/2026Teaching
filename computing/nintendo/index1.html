World 1
Year 7 · Stage 4 · CT4 Outcomes · 1 Semester

Origins — From Pixels to Play

Reverse-engineer Nintendo's history, build a 2D platformer in MakeCode Arcade, wire a micro:bit as a physical controller, and design your first Amiibo-style figurine for 3D printing.

Scratch MakeCode Arcade micro:bit TinkerCAD HTML / CSS
🍄 Lesson 1 · Analyse

How Mario Began

Deconstruct Nintendo's history from Donkey Kong to Switch. How did enterprise and innovation shape the games we play?

 📐 Lesson 2 · Design

Paper Prototype Lab

Design your platformer on paper. Identify mechanics, rules, and player feedback loops — then map them to pseudocode.

 🎮 Lessons 3–6 · Build

MakeCode Arcade Platformer

Build a 2D side-scroller with movement, gravity, collectibles, and a win state — blocks bridging to JavaScript.

 📡 Lessons 7–8 · Extend

micro:bit Controller

Wire a micro:bit as a physical game controller using buttons and the accelerometer. Hardware meets software.

 🖨️ Lessons 9–10 · Make

TinkerCAD Amiibo Design

Design an original character figurine and prepare it for 3D printing. Your first physical product ships in World 3.

 📓 Ongoing · Reflect

Project Notebook

Document every sprint, decision, and iteration. Your notebook is your evidence of thinking, not a polished report.

World 2
Year 9 · Stage 5 · CT5 Outcomes · 1 Semester

Super Maker — 2D Platformer

Pitch a 2D platformer to Nintendo AU using Godot 4 and GDScript. Study real UX decisions from store to game, and collect and analyse player feedback data to drive your iterative design process.

Godot 4 GDScript Python Figma HTML / CSS / JS
🔍 Lessons 1–2 · Analyse

Deconstruct a Godot Project

Read an existing 2D platformer in Godot 4. Trace the scene tree, annotate the code, and identify the design decisions behind every mechanic.

 📝 Lesson 3 · Design

Game Design Document

Produce a full GDD with NSW structured English pseudocode for your core loop, player mechanics, and level structure.

 🏃 Sprint 1 · Build

Movement & Physics

Player movement, gravity, collision detection, and a tilemap — pseudocode to GDScript with checkpoint testing.

 👾 Sprint 2 · Build

Enemies & Collectibles

State-machine enemies, coin systems, scoring, and player feedback via sound and visual effects. Iterative development in action.

 🗺️ Sprint 3 · Build

Level Design & Polish

Multiple levels, UI screens, sound design, win and loss states. Final playable build for assessment.

 🎨 EIS · Designing for UX

From Store to Game

Analyse Nintendo's full user journey — eShop to in-game UI. Apply UX heuristics to your own game's design decisions.

 📊 EIS · Analysing Data

Player Feedback & Data

Design a feedback survey, collect playtesting data, visualise the findings, and use them to justify your design iterations.

 📓 Ongoing · Reflect

Project Notebook

Sprint checkpoint entries, CPD-referenced self-assessment, and your full portfolio evidence for the assessment task.

World 3
Year 10 · Stage 5 · CT5 Outcomes · 1 Semester

Galaxy & Beyond — 3D Game + e-Store

Build a 3D game in Unity 6 (Galaxy-style gravity or Kart World racing), design and ship a Nintendo-branded e-store web app, and pitch your complete portfolio to a studio panel for CPD assessment.

Unity 6 C# ShaderGraph HTML / CSS / JS Figma TinkerCAD GitHub Pages
🔭 Lessons 1–2 · Analyse

Deconstruct a Unity 3D Project

Trace physics, camera systems, and shaders in an existing Unity 6 scene. Build vocabulary for your Game Design Document.

 🌌 Strand A · Path 1

Galaxy-Style 3D Game

Gravity-shifting mechanics, orbital movement, collectibles, and custom shaders in Unity URP. Push the Grade A ceiling.

 🏎️ Strand A · Path 2

Kart World Racing

Track design, vehicle physics, lap timing, and opponent AI. Choose your path — both meet the same CPD outcome ceiling.

 ✨ Strand A · Visual

Shader Series

Custom materials in Unity ShaderGraph — unlit colour, textures, UV animation, and toon shading for your game world.

 🛒 Strand B · e-Store · UX

Nintendo e-Store: Design

Audit the real Nintendo store. Wireframe and prototype in Figma — every decision driven by heuristic analysis.

 💻 Strand B · e-Store · Build

Nintendo e-Store: Build

HTML/CSS/JS product listings, JSON catalogue, filter and sort, localStorage cart. Deploy to GitHub Pages.

 🖨️ Strand B · Physical Product

Amiibo Redesign

Revisit your Year 7 figurine — redesign in TinkerCAD, print on the Bambu P2S, photograph it, and list it in your e-store.

 📓 Ongoing · Reflect

Project Notebook

Full D&P framework evidence, CPD self-assessment across all grade descriptors, and notes for your studio panel pitch.
