Galaxy-Style 3D Game

Gravity doesn't behave the way it does on your kart track. Here, your player bends and shifts gravity between orbiting platforms, collects star fragments, and survives increasingly complex zones. Three sprints — the first builds movement and gravity, the second adds collectibles and feedback, the third finishes with multiple zones, UI, and a full win/lose loop.

Your Mission

You're building a short vertical slice: a player who can shift which surface counts as "down," moving between platforms that orbit a central point or drift in open space. The core loop is simple to describe and hard to get feeling right — that's exactly why it's a strong Stage 5 project. Sprint 1 gets movement and gravity working. Sprint 2 gives the player something to do (collect, score, react to feedback). Sprint 3 turns it into a real, finishable game with multiple zones and clear win/lose states.

Before you start Sprint 1, make sure your Lessons 1–2 Game Design Document has pseudocode for at least your movement system — Sprint 1 builds directly from it.
